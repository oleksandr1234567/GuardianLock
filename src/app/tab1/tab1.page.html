This is an app to help you to keep track of your passwords.

Do you strugle to remember your passwords? Do you have have a lot of accounts and you use always the same password, because you're afraid to forget one? Do you use weak passwords to not forget them?


GuardianLock is the solution!


In GuardianLock you can easily save all your passwords and in a secure database. This way you can always just come back and copy it. So there is no need to create weak passwords. You can create complex and safe passwords and just save them in your GuardianLock, and then come fetch them at any time!
